3.4 Magnitude Earthquake VIRGINIA - 1st September 2011





A magnitude 3.4 earthquake has struck Virginia at a depth of 4.9 km ( 3 miles), the quake hit at 09:09:37 UTC Thursday 1st September 2011.
The epicenter was 52 km (31 miles) Southwest of Charlottesville, Virginia
No Reports of Damage or Injuries at this time
