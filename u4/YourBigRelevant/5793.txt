3.4 Magnitude Earthquake VIRGINIA - 24th August 2011





A magnitude 3.4 earthquake has struck Virginia at a depth of just 4.9 km  ( 3 miles), the quake hit at 04:45:26 UTC Wednesday 24th August 2011.
The epicenter was 44 km (27 miles) ESE of Charlottesville, Virginia
No Reports of Damage or Injuries at this time
